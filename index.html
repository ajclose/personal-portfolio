Personal Profile

Hi, my name is Alex Close and I’m a Web Developer with a background in Mechanical and Quality Engineering. Five years ago, I started my career with Parker Hannifin—a global design and manufacturing company specializing in motion and control technologies. I started as a Quality Engineer Trainee and worked my way up to Senior Quality Engineer where I was responsible for helping customers solve product problems, improve supplier quality metrics, and improve internal processes through Root Cause Analysis, Lean Manufacturing, and Six-Sigma principles.

While I enjoyed the analytical and problem-solving aspects of that job, I found myself wanting to do something more creative. I decided to make a change and switched gears in search of a career where I could have more of an impact solving real-world problems and finding a better way of doing things.

After careful consideration, I decided to dive head-first into the world of web development and enrolled in The Iron Yard’s immersive web development boot camp. In this program, I learned the fundamentals of front-end development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the fundamentals of back-end development using Node.js, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB, and completed a specialization in Ruby on Rails.

I’ve found that I love using my new set of skills to develop creative and easy-to-use solutions for complex problems. I believe in doing things the right way because I know that it will result in a better product for the customer. If you combine that passion with my critical thinking skills and real-world experience in solving problems for co-workers, customers, and suppliers, I believe that I’d be a valued and successful candidate at any organization.

I’m always interested in building new relationships with those in the industry and learning from other like-minded people.
